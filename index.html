Conflict Prevention and Mediation



There could be multiple reasons why conflict arises, like due to ideological differences, sociocultural differences, opposing interests, etc. Although, on an individual level it is insignificant but it can bring devastating impact if the same conflict arises between the countries. That's why it is important to prevent or mediate conflict before it transforms into a catastrophic war.
